Work in progress

* Use two-part epoxy for the TAP magnets...superglue will let loose and ruin your day.  Drifting z-offset or sudden changes of .6mm might indicate a problem with the magnets.
* Read the R8 TAP errata.  The official manual is several years old.
* For the Phaetus Dragon High flow hotend, there is an updated toolhead STL on printables:  https://www.printables.com/model/625623-phaetus-dragon-hf-printhead-mount-for-stealthburne
* For the MagicPhoenix CBT kit, Use Cable_Cover_EBB.stl and Main_Body_EBB.stl from BTT’s [EBB/EBB SB2240_2209 CAN](https://github.com/bigtreetech/EBB/tree/master/EBB%20SB2240_2209%20CAN/STL) repo for the clockwork 2 extruder.  These are a better fit for the toolhead board.  There have been some filename changes so the MPX wiki docs are out of date.  
* You probably need some active cooling on the TMC 2209s even during initial setup.  Prioritize printing the fan mount from the Voron /skirts folder so you don't have to balance a fan under the printer.  I would contest the "cosmetic" status of that part.
* These [rollback stands](https://www.printables.com/model/456448-service-rockers-voron-v2-vtv1) are fantastic for electronics access if you have a big printer like a 350.
